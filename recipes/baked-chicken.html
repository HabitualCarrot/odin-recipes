Baked Chicken

Description

Who doesn't love a juicy chicken dinner!? Well this simple and easy recipe will be sure to get your mouth watering, and satisfy the whole family for dinner!

Ingredients

  • parchment paper, or aluminium foil, 12x18 inches (30.5 x 46 cm)
  • 1 chicken breast
  • one-half red pepper, sliced
  • one-half yellow pepper
  • one-half red onion, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons salsa
  • 1 teaspoon taco seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon mexican cheese blend
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil salt
  • pepper, to taste
