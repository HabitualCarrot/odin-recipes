Grilled Cheese

Description

Grilled Cheese, an American classic. Whether it be from your grandmothers kitchen or a gourmet resturaunt, this simple meal is the ultimate comfort food. There are many many many ways of preparing this dish, but I prefer to keep it simple.

Today I will be covering my preferred method, the way my grandmother taught me. Let's dive in and see what we'll need!

Ingredients

choice of bread and cheese is up to your preference, but do note that thinly sliced breads may not hold up to the heat as well

  • butter
  • white american cheese
  • mayo
  • sliced bread
  • optional:sliced oven roasted turkey

Steps

  1. bring your skillet to medium/high heat
  2. butter one side of a slice of bread and place it butter side down on the skillet
  3. add your cheese (one to two slices)
  4. optional: add sliced oven roasted turkey
  5. apply mayo to one side of slice of bread and place on top (mayo side down)
  6. butter the top slice of bread
  7. cook until bottom slice is golden brown, and then flip
  8. continue until cheese is melted
